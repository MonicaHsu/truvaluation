SF real estate is a
'Seller's Market'.

There has been a continuous drop in the number of homes for sale on the market in SF. With an overabundance of buyers, many homes receive multiple offers.

Most homes in SF
are underpriced.

On average, homes in SF sell for 7% above the asking price. Sellers price houses low in order to drive up demand and create bidding wars between buyers.

Home sale prices
can be predicted.

TruValuation predicts home sale prices in SF with a median error of 1.5%. Using this tool, buyers can determine whether a home is fairly priced and how much the home is likely to sell for.
