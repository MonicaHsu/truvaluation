TruValuation predicts home sale prices with a median error of 1.5%

There are many variables that go into the prediction, but the four most important variables are described below:

1. Transit Score: This is a measure of the proximity to public transit. In San Francisco, this is the most important variable in predicting sale price.

2. Year Built: The year the house was built is the second most important variable in TruValuation's model.

3. Walk Score: This is a measure of the walkability of a neighborhood, proximity to restaurants, grocery stores and pedestrian friendliness.

4. Popularity Score: This is a metric developed by TruValuation to measure the online presence of a house on real estate websites.
